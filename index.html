JOIN US THIS FALL!

Autumn is finally here, and there’s no better way to celebrate than a trip to Pumpkin Farm Patch! Spend the day wandering through rows of bright orange pumpkins waiting to be picked, then challenge yourself and your friends to make it through our winding corn maze.

Families can enjoy tractor-pulled hayrides, while little ones will love our petting zoo and pumpkin painting station. Don’t forget to stop by our Harvest Café for a warm cup of apple cider, fresh-baked pumpkin bread, and other fall treats made with love.

Whether you’re searching for the perfect carving pumpkin, looking to capture cozy seasonal photos, or just hoping to soak up the crisp autumn air, Pumpkin Farm has something for everyone to enjoy.

We can’t wait to welcome you this weekend for a celebration full of laughter, tradition, and unforgettable fall memories!
